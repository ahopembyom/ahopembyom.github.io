What does it solve?

The produce from small farms or homesteads are of a superior quality. However, the traceability of the produce obtained from multiple farm units and the erratic nature of the supply of these produce from the homesteads have deterred manufacturers and markets from depending on this supply chain of produce. aHOPE provides for a system of traceability of produce right from the individual homestead and a clear line of sight of the supply status of diverse range produce. Additionally, aHOPE utilizes the advantages of a defined and transparent pricing visible throughout the transaction and the option of e-payment accounts for a complete supply chain management package for the manufacturing units and markets.

Connect with Nearby Aggregators to Purchase from Local Farmers

View crops available for purchase from nearby aggregators and their prices. Choose your desired crop from any of the listed aggregators. Traceability ensures that each batch received from the aggregator can be traced down to the corresponding farmer.

View Farmer Receipts

You can connect with Aggregators for a continuous supply of produce for your unit – in this case, a triangulation check of the crop purchased by the Aggregator is needed. aHOPE lets you view farmer acknowledgements or receipts issued by your selected aggregator to the farmer to ensure that the required crops are being purchased as per your requirements.

Request Drop Off

Choose the crop, quantity and convenient date for drop off. You can also choose to pick up the crops yourself!

Pay the Aggregators Online!

Once the required crops have been purchased and verified, you can pay the aggregator online instantly!
