What does it solve?

Farmers often have diverse crops grown in their farms generally of small volume. These homesteads and small farms are geographically distributed making procurement and aggregation of these crops a difficult task. aHOPE solves this problem by enabling the Agent (or aggregator) to act as a as a single-window service provider for the small farmer. By connecting the supply (Farmers) with the demand (Manufacturing units), aHOPE’s Agent application establishes traceability and fair pricing. Equipped with information of both the seller and the buyer, the data managed by the Agent application will also help anticipate and establish linkages with banks for working capital arrangements. The aHOPE application aims at empowering the Agent ecosystem to function in a transparent manner rather than removing the dependence on the Agent by linking the farmer directly to the markets.

Know your Farmers and Collect Crops

Answer pickup or drop requests from farmers. Through access to a database of farmers linked to you, understand the current market pricings to stay competitive.

Receive Orders from aHOPE verified Manufacturing units

List the items you are willing to sell and receive bulk orders from nearby registered Manufacturing units. You may list processed or unprocessed items and your price for the same!

Logistical Support through aHOPE verified Drivers

Connect with registered drivers nearby who will pick up crops from your farmers. Similarly, drivers can be connected with to drop your items at the Manufacturing Unit.

Pay and Get Paid Online!

Pay the farmer and get paid by the Manufacturing unit online using the aHOPE application.
